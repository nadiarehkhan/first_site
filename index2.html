About Studilicious

STUDILICIOUS brings you healthy, nutritious, quick and easy recipes to suit your busy student lifestyle.

What makes us different?

• All our recipes are £5 or under. • 30 minutes is all it takes for your delicious meal.

Why did we start Studilicious?

• We are students with specific dietary preferences living in one of the most expensive cities in the world. • We have a passion for homemade healthy food!

It's all love!
